Regina Seibel

PhD candidate in Economics

University of Zurich, Department of Economics

I am a fourth-year PhD student in Economics at the University of Zurich under the supervision of professor Armin Schmutzler and professor Gregory Crawford. I completed both my bachelor’s (2016) and master’s (2017) degree in Economics at the Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität in Munich. During my master’s degree I attended PhD courses at the Univerisity of California, Berkeley. My research focuses on studying anti-competitive firm behavior and evaluating policies with respect to their ability to prevent or reduce consumer harm, while taking into account effects on innovation and investment activity.
